The Safir top-scores for materials, safety, manageability, cold protection, ventilation and comfort. Three large, net-fitted air intakes are standard. The door is mosquito-protected and it is fitted with extra buttons, in case of emergency.

And it is so convenient. For example, our patented In-Tent Vent™ system lets you adjust the ventilator cap to regulate the temperature without even leaving your sleeping bag. So, you can stay in bed even when the sun comes up and starts to warm the tent.

You’ll have loads of space and full standing room in the Safir, plus the snug togetherness of that open fire if you want it.

The reinforced edging and other details are there to give you the best possible resistance to the kind of events that often break a tent. They give you peace of mind and extend the life span of your investment.
